
    Frequently Asked Questions about Generative AI (Text Version)

    1. What is Generative AI?
    - Generative AI refers to AI systems capable of generating new content such as text, images, videos, etc., 
      based on learned patterns from input data.

    2. What are Large Language Models (LLMs)?
    - Large Language Models (LLMs) are a type of Generative AI specifically designed to generate human-like text based 
      on vast datasets of text input. Examples include GPT-3, GPT-4, and other transformer-based models.

    3. What are the applications of Generative AI?
    - Generative AI can be used for various applications such as content creation (writing articles, generating poetry), 
      image synthesis, video editing, code generation, music composition, and more.

    4. What is the difference between Generative AI and traditional AI?
    - Traditional AI typically relies on predefined rules to perform tasks, while Generative AI is designed to create 
      new content by learning from data patterns.
