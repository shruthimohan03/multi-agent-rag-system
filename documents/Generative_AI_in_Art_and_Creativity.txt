
    Generative AI in Art and Creativity

    1. AI-Generated Art
    - Generative AI has been used to create original pieces of artwork, from paintings to sculptures, by learning from existing 
      art styles.

    2. Music Composition
    - Generative AI models can create original music compositions in various genres, using learned patterns from music theory 
      and composition.

    3. Writing and Poetry
    - Generative AI can assist writers by creating story ideas, generating character dialogues, and even writing full poems and stories.

    4. Collaborative Creativity
    - Artists and creatives can use generative AI tools to collaborate, enhancing their own creative output through AI-generated suggestions.
